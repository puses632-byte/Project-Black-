# PROJECT: BLACK

> Evidence Before Belief.

PROJECT: BLACK is an independent research collective dedicated to investigating complex questions through evidence, critical thinking, and interdisciplinary collaboration.

## Mission

We seek individuals who:

- Question assumptions
- Verify information through evidence
- Conduct original research
- Collaborate respectfully
- Pursue truth with intellectual honesty

## Research Areas

- Open-Source Intelligence (OSINT)
- Cybersecurity
- History
- Ancient Civilizations
- Comparative Religion
- Symbolism
- Philosophy
- Psychology
- Artificial Intelligence
- Consciousness Research
